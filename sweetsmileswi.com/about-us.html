About Our Dental Office – Grafton, WI

We Make Going to the Dentist Easy

We like to do things a little differently here at Sweet Smiles Family Dentistry. Rather than sending you running all over town with referrals, our dentists are able to provide almost every service you can imagine right here in our dental office. They’re happy to take care of every member of your family, regardless of their age! We also have hours that work for busy people’s schedules, and our payment options can help absolutely everyone easily afford dental care. We speak several languages as well for clear, easy communication, including English, Spanish, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Mandarin, and Arabic. When you partner with us, you’ll never have to worry about you and your loved ones' teeth again.

Convenient Hours & Locations

Sweet Smiles Family Dentistry has locations in Milwaukee and Grafton, so no matter where you are, you’ll never have to travel far to come see us. This makes it easy to keep up with regular appointments, plus you’ll have nearly instant access to emergency dental care if you ever require it! And, we’re open in the evening as well as Saturdays, so you won’t ever have to miss work or school for an appointment.

Contact Us

Dental Emergencies Welcome

Unexpected toothaches or sudden dental injuries are always stressful, but you only need to do one thing to get the immediate, professional care you deserve: call us. When you do, we’ll schedule you to be seen right away so we can figure out your problem and stop the pain as soon as possible. We’ll be able to see you much faster than the ER or an urgent care clinic, provide higher quality service, and we’re much more affordable as well!

Learn About Emergency Dentistry

Dental Insurance Accepted

Dental insurance offers an easy way to make your dental care more affordable, and at Sweet Smiles Family Dentistry, we’re happy to accept and maximize most major PPO plans. Whether it’s time for your child’s checkup or you’re interested in repairing/replacing some of your teeth, our team will know how to use your plan so you can get what you need and save as much as possible.

Learn About Dental Insurance

State-of-the-Art Dental Technology

Dental care is faster, more comfortable, and more efficient than ever thanks to our highly-advanced technology. We can provide same-day crowns with CEREC, quickly make dentures and mouthguards with our 3D printer, and even take impressions completely digitally without dental putty! Every patient is also provided with an iPad so they can watch Netflix during their appointments to help them fly by.

Learn About Dental Technology

One Stop Dental Services

If a certain treatment can help a person achieve a healthier, more beautiful smile, then you can trust we’re able to provide it. Checkups and cleanings for the entire family? We got you and your children covered! Have a few teeth that need to be repaired or replaced? Custom-made restorations are easily available. Simply want whiter, more attractive teeth? Our cosmetic services can give you a smile you’re always eager to show off. Here, you just walk in, and we’ll take care of the rest.
